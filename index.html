A Helping Hand

Saving humanity and saving the earth are one and the same struggle.
It is increasingly clear to many that capitalism, with its overarching drive
to accumulate and expand, and treating nature as a commodity in the same
way it treats humanity and human needs, is fundamentally
unable to save the environment.Earth is the only planet which can sustain
life on it. We do not have a planet B which we can move onto.
This makes it all the more serious to save
the earth and save our lives.
